Handyman Tips for Cibolo

Protect trim from creekside humidity

Homes near Cibolo Creek and the greenbelts often experience higher humidity that can swell trim and door casings‚Äîkeep caulked seams sealed and choose paints with mildew resistance.

Guard siding from sun-faded south walls

Newer subdivisions with wide-open views get intense afternoon sun; use UV-rated exterior caulk and plan repainting cycles sooner on the south and west elevations to prevent warping or chalking.

Control dust along farm roads

Properties near the farm-to-market roads can collect fine dust inside; replace HVAC filters more frequently and add weatherstripping around exterior doors to keep interiors cleaner.
